Our Services

We provide full interior design services from concept to reality.

Interior Design

We conceive spaces. We select fabrics. We forge textures. We visualize colours. We create furniture. We imagine lighting. At Ele Interior Design, we do it all for you. New or renovation. Villa, penthouse or commercial space. We are creators of interior spaces.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

We ensure that the project we designed for you becomes real. We procure and import material, select the best contractors and manage them up to completion so that we can guarantee that your dream becomes reality as planned.

FURNITURE AND DÉCOR

Our clients are demanding, we thank them for that. Because we love to take care of every detail and create new and unmatched designs, we are experienced in customizing furniture, chandelier, wall papers, curtains and more. Because you are unique, we make sure your project’s identity is too.
