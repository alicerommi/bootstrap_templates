What We Offer

We can eliminate and protect your property from all of the below pests and more using a variety of unique and safe application methods.

Cockroaches including German Cockroaches

All species of cockroaches can be exterminated including any major german cockroach infestations.

Spider

All spiders including your deadly red back and funnel web spiders can be exterminated.

Fleas

Keep your family and pets safe with a detailed flea treatment. End of lease treatments can also be carried out along with a post-service report.

Rodents

All species of rodents that include rats and mice can be treated via a variety of application methods. Advice will be given on the best approach. Rodent bait station installations are also available

Bedbugs

“Sleep tight, don’t let the bed bugs bite!” – It’s not just a goodnight phrase, its also a precaution in the pest world. Waking up with bites? Notice bed bugs on your mattress or in your bedroom?

Wasps/Bees

Active Wasp Nests and Bee Hives can be produced at any time at any location. Leave it to the professionals to eradicate these nests without causing any harm to yourself or your family.

Bird Control

A variety of bird control applications can be carried out such as bird spiking to prevent birds from nesting or harbouring in certain areas causing high risk of bird lice occurring

Ants

Ant infestations particularly in kitchens can be a menace. Explore our detailed ant treatment today.
