What is a Combi boiler?

Combi boilers are the most popular boilers in the UK. Combi boilers allow you to have water heating and central heating.

What is an ErP label?

ErP stands for Energy related products. The ErP label shows how efficient the boiler is and how much energy it uses.

What it’s the most efficient boiler I can get?

The most efficient boiler should be A+++ ErP labelled.

You must service your boiler annually

What pressure should my boiler be?

When the heating system is cool, the pressure should be between 1 and 1.5 bar on the pressure gauge. If pressure gauge is below 0.5 bar, water has been lost from the system and must be replaced.

What is a flue?

A flue is a pipe, like a chimney, that carried waste from the boiler outside. You can find your flue exit on the wall or roof of your house.

Does my boiler come with warranty?

Our boilers come with 10 years warranty.

What is ATAG ONE?

The easy-to-navigate ATAG ONE Controller is connected through the internet to provide a bespoke, programmable heating control that operates both inside and outside of the home, using a smart device, tablet or desktop PC.

Where can I download the ONE controller app?

What is a System boiler?

System boiler heat your radiators directly and store hot water in a storage cylinder. They’re usually installed in an airing cupboard or garage. They heat a large volume of water and then store it until you use it. They are less efficient than Combi boilers.

What is a Standard boiler?

Standard boilers are known as Conventional or Regular boilers. They store hot water in a cylinder like the System boilers, however they are fed by separate cold-water tank which is usually in the loft. There’s another separate tank, called a ‘feed and expansion cistern’ usually in the loft too.

What is a water treatment pack?

The water treatment pack provides all you need to maintain system efficiency and protect your boiler.

Do you need anything to do anything before the installer arrives?

Before your installer arrive, please clear away around the old boiler or where you want your boiler to be installed.

What do I do with the boiler that has a fault code 118?

Error 118 could mean that the system pressure is too low and you may see that the system pressure gauge indicates that the pressure in the system is below the 1 bar pressure.
