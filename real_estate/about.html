Company Info

The Company was incorporated in 1951. Its original name was: Inmobiliaria SRL Its main activities were the sale of buildings in the Central and Northern zones of the City of Buenos Aires and the administration of Consortiums and Rents. In parallel, the firm promoted the construction of buildings subject to the regime of Law 13,512 Horizontal Property, highlighting important works carried out in the following locations: Paraguay and Libertador, Cordoba and Florida Av., Esmeralda and Corrientes, Charcas and Agüero, Manuela Pedraza 220, Av. Belgrano and Pozos, Agüero and Juncal, City of La Paz and J. Azurduy.

Since 1980, the corporate structure of the company has been transformed, which changes its name and is now called RR Franchino SRL The firm increases its real estate activities aimed at developing real estate ventures and stops managing consortiums. At the same time, he continues his activity in the real estate intermediation of buying and selling and locations of all types of real estate: departments, houses, offices, industrial buildings, land, etc.
